About Me

Hi, my name is Alex Hillman, a Games Technology University student at UWE Bristol.

I am currently working on numerous projects both academic and personal.

My academic work varies, from an old school first person shooter created with DirectX11 to image classification in Python, a group created Atari-style demake of The Binding of Isaac to a dissertation project in virtual reality.

Many personal projects, mostly for Unity, have been created to test a range of ideas and functionality such as networking and AI pathfinding or just to experience the development of different game types, be that a 2D side scrolling platformer or a 3D first-person shooter.

You can find most of my projects on my GitHub account.
